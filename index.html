Privacy Policy

Better Logistics (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Better Logistics mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide and improve our services. This includes:

  • Personal Information: such as your name, phone number, and other details you provide during registration or while using the app.

2. How We Use the Information

We use the collected information to:

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as required to deliver our services (for example, sharing delivery details with assigned drivers) or when required by law.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Your Choices

You can choose to disable location access from your device settings at any time. However, this may affect some features of the App such as delivery tracking or location-based services.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any updates will be posted within the App with an updated “Effective Date.”
